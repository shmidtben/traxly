The problem

Finishing a track is the hardest part.

You've listened too many times. Your producer friends say different things. You have no idea if it actually lands.

01

Everyone gives different feedback

Your producer friends hear the mixdown. Your non-musician friends are being polite. Neither tells you if it connects emotionally.

02

You've lost perspective on your own track

After 200 listens you can't hear it anymore. You need fresh ears — specifically ears that live in your genre.

03

Promotion tools don't answer "is it ready?"

Existing platforms tell you if a curator will playlist it. They don't tell you if it's finished. That's a completely different question.

04

Nothing fits the creative workflow

Feedback tools feel like PR services. You need something that lives in the studio process — the way Splice does for samples.
